WHO ARE WE

THE “MY CHANCE” PROJECT WAS LAUNCHED IN 2025 WITH THE SUPPORT OF THE MINISTRY OF CULTURE AND SPORTS OF THE REPUBLIC OF KAZAKHSTAN. OUR GOAL IS TO GIVE CHILDREN THE OPPORTUNITY TO CHANGE THEIR LIVES THROUGH SPORT. WE STARTED WITH TRIATHLON, AND IN 2026 WE PLAN TO ADD BASKETBALL AND SWIMMING, EXPANDING THE PROGRAM TO MAKE IT LONGER AND MORE ENGAGING. OUR PARTICIPANTS ARE CHILDREN FROM BOARDING SCHOOLS, SCHOOLS FOR LOW-INCOME FAMILIES, AND CHILDREN WITH SPECIAL NEEDS.
